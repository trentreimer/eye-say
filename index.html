This site uses side-to-side eye movement to operate a simple switch-based typewriter.

Instructions Typewriter

The typewriter needs a well-lit room, a webcam, and a wide enough screen that the camera can easily see when your eyes move from one side to the other.

Laptops work, even cheap Chromebooks if they are newer. Tablets work if they are optimized for landscape mode, e.g. iPad Pro and tablets with a camera on the long edge.

Current versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge are all compatible.

Privacy

The video stream used for eye tracking does not leave your computer.
