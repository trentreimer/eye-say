Requirements

To use the typewriter you need an up-to-date web browser, a well-lit room, a webcam, and a large enough screen that the camera can see your eyes move from one side to the other.

Laptops work, even cheap ones if they are newer. Tablets struggle unless they are optimized for landscape mode. Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge are all compatible.
